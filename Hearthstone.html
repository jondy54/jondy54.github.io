Hearthstone is a free-to-play digital card game developed and run by Blizzard Entertainment in 2014. Hearthstone is inspired by the characters and lore of Blizzard's Warcraft franchise and is playable on Windows, Mac, iPhone, and Android.
Hearthstone is a turn-based card game between two opponents with constructed 30-card decks. At the start of the game, each player begins with one mana crystal which is used to pay for playing the card.During each of your turns, you gain one additional mana crystal until you reach a total of 10. Both players start at 30 health points and the objective of the game is to use your card in order to reduce the opponent's health points to zero. Playing requires both skill and a bit of luck as some mechanics have randomness attached to them.

Test your luck! How many attempts will it take for you to get a 10.
